# Taxonomy and Escalation Rubric

## Categories

* **Incorrect account filing** - the status of a customers account has been set incorrectly

* **Disputed/unauthorized account** - the consumer contests that an item is legitimately theirs/authorized - distinct from incorrect filing, which is a genuine data error on a real account

* **Lack of action in a reasonable timeframe** - no responses, no actions taken after commited to verbally or in writing, no responses or action after follow ups

* **Closed dispute without a valid reason** - the dispute is closed without addresssing the problem

* **Predatory practices** - descriptions of predatory business practices

* **Unable to make contact through established channels** - the customer is unable to contact the organisation through the channels advertised by the brand

## Escalation Risk Signals

### Low - slight inconvenience for the customer

* Lack of or delayed responses

### Medium - significant gap between the quality expectation and the delivered service

* Subpar service quality

### High - loss of money, time, reputation or health

* Rights violations
* Abusive behaviour
* Financial hardship
* Credit score damage

## Tiebreaker Rule

If a complaint is between two tiers, move up to the higher one to prevent missed escalations.
